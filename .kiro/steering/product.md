# Product Overview

ARL Hockey is a landing page for the Adult Recreational League, a co-ed recreational hockey community serving Burlington, Hamilton, Milton, and Oakville in Ontario, Canada.

## Purpose

The site serves as a central hub directing users to two main destinations:
- **ARL Hockey League** (rookiehockey.ca) - Social co-ed hockey league for beginners and experienced players
- **ARL Board** (board.arl.hockey) - League governance and administrative information

## Key Features

- Dual-destination landing page with clear navigation cards
- Photo gallery showcasing league activities
- Theme support (light/dark/system)
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
- Non-competitive, beginner-friendly positioning

## Target Audience

Adult recreational hockey players of all skill levels looking for a social, non-contact hockey experience in the Greater Toronto Area.
